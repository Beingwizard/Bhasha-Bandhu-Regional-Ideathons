## Bhasha Bandhu Hackathon  

Welcome to the **Bhasha Bandhu Hackathon**, a pioneering event presented by **Bhashini** in partnership with **Microsoft**. This collaboration brings together the technological excellence of Microsoft and Bhashini's mission to bridge **India's linguistic digital divide**.  

Bhashini, endorsed by the **Ministry of Electronics & Information Technology (MeitY)**, provides open-source AI models and datasets through the **Digital India Bhashini initiative**. Our aim is to foster an ecosystem of **innovation and digital empowerment** that enhances accessibility and inclusion across India.

### üéÅ What We Will Provide:  
‚úÖ Participants will work on **4 different problem statements**

### üìå What We Expect:  
‚úÖ **In-person participation**  
‚úÖ **Hackathon space** to ensure a smooth experience for participants & mentors  
‚úÖ **Idea submissions** by every participant  
‚úÖ **Basic AV arrangements** (mic, projector)  

### üìå Process to Organise the Hackathon  

‚úÖ Look after a date in February (before 28th February) when we can plan the Ideathon (4-hour quick idea submission)  
‚úÖ Shortlisted ideas will be forwarded to the next step and they will get time till 22nd March to work upon their ideas  
‚úÖ Regional winners will be declared on the day of Ideathon and will be given some swags for encouragement, but shortlisted ideas aren't limited to winners.  
For example: We can have 3 regional winners but 10 shortlisted ideas for the hackathon  
‚úÖ Offline Hack Day with Bhasini & Microsoft officials on 22nd March  

### üìå Ideathon Day ~ 4hrs

‚úÖ 30 minutes - Mentors explaining about Bhashini API, Open API, GitHub Copilot (best practices, how to use those)  
‚úÖ 5 minutes - Mentors explaining about the Problem Statement  
‚úÖ 25 minutes - QnA  
‚úÖ Next 2 hours - Brainstorming Ideas, creating ppt  
‚úÖ 1.5 hour - Mentor evaluating the scores for Regional Winners  
‚úÖ 30 minutes - Regional Winner Announcement & Facilitation  

### üìå What participants have to submit on Ideathon Day

‚úÖ Ppt with following details: Problem Statement, Solution Statement, Use of Bhashini API and Open AI, other Tech Stack used, Unique Selling Proposition, Business Potential  
‚úÖ Idea Title  
‚úÖ Brief Explanation about the Idea submitted  
‚úÖ How the problem is being solved with Bhashini  
‚úÖ Problem Statement Chosen
